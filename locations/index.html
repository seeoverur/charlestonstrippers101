Pricing and Booking Considerations

The cost of hiring strippers in Charleston varies based on several factors, including the performer's experience level, the length of the performance, travel distance to your venue, and any special requests or customizations. Generally, prices range from a few hundred to several hundred dollars per performer, with premium entertainers and extended shows commanding higher rates.

Most Charleston entertainment companies require advance booking, especially during peak seasons like spring and summer when bachelorette parties and celebrations are most frequent. Booking at least two to four weeks in advance is recommended, though some companies can accommodate last-minute requests depending on performer availability. Peak times such as Friday and Saturday evenings, holiday weekends, and special events like Charleston's numerous festivals often require even earlier booking.

Payment terms vary by company, but many require a deposit at the time of booking with the balance due before or immediately after the performance. Be sure to clarify what's included in the quoted price, such as travel fees, performance duration, costume changes, and any additional services. Professional companies provide clear written agreements that outline all terms and expectations.
